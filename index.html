Work Samples

Beacon Manor

Role: Gameplay Programmer / AI Systems / Interaction Systems / Map Development

Engine: Unreal Engine 5

Tools: C++, Blueprints, Perforce, Jira

Beacon Manor is a first-person horror mystery game where the player explores a haunted manor while using a flashlight as a survival tool, investigation tool, and puzzle-solving mechanic.

  • Implemented ghost AI states including patrol, chase, attack, stunned, frozen, and return-to-patrol.
  • Built flashlight interaction support for slowing, freezing, and affecting ghost enemies.
  • Created designer-ready ghost Blueprint presets for different colors and behavior types.
  • Worked on map development for the library, pool, bathroom, kitchen, dining area, greenhouse entrance, and secret room flow.
